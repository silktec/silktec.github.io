A War-Torn Region
Not Officially at War

A well-armed and murderous separatist movement has caused
Southern Thailand to be the 2nd most bomb-plagued area
in the world after Afghanistan. Since 2004, more than 5,000 innocent
civilians have been killed, and 9,000 more have been injured
- many of them children.

Designs that
Save Lives

We provide specially designed bomb-shrapnel-proof silk
clothing that is a scientific breakthrough and life saver.
This specially weaved silk is resistant to the metal pieces
loaded into homemade bombs, which means this clothing
decreases the likelihood of serious injury or death
if a bomb goes off nearby. Additionally, we create
garments that are fashionable and wearable
in everyday life.
